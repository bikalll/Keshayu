Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our organic hair solutions

How long does shipping take?

We typically process orders within 1-2 business days. Shipping within Nepal takes 2-5 business days, while international shipping takes 7-14 business days depending on your location.

Are your products really organic?

Yes! All our products are certified organic and made with natural ingredients. We never use sulfates, parabens, synthetic fragrances, or artificial colors in any of our formulations.

Can I get a personalized hair care routine?

Absolutely! Our hair experts can create a customized routine based on your hair type and concerns. Contact us with details about your hair goals, and we'll recommend the perfect products for you.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders?

Yes, we offer special pricing for salons, spas, and retailers. Please contact our wholesale department at wholesale@keshaayu.com for pricing and minimum order requirements.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, contact us for a full refund or exchange. Note that shipping costs are non-refundable.
