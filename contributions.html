Our Social Commitment

KeshAayu is built on principles of empowerment, sustainability, and community support. Discover how we're making a difference.

Entrepreneurship Training

We provide business training and seed funding to help women start their own enterprises. Our program has launched 50+ small businesses in the past two years.

Girls' Education Fund

We sponsor education for over 200 girls annually, providing tuition, supplies, and mentorship to help them break the cycle of poverty.

Skill Development

We offer vocational training in traditional crafts, organic farming, and beauty services to provide women with sustainable income opportunities.

Our Women-Led Workforce

At KeshAayu, we're proud that 90% of our team are talented women who bring passion and expertise to every aspect of our business.

Support for Single Mothers

We provide flexible schedules, childcare support, and specialized benefits to help single mothers thrive in their careers.

Career Development

Through training and mentorship programs, we empower our female employees to advance into leadership positions.

Sustainable Livelihoods

We partner with women farmers and herb collectors, providing fair prices and sustainable harvesting training.

Our Community Initiatives

At KeshAayu, we believe in giving back to the community and nurturing our planet. Explore the events and initiatives we're proud to have been a part of.

MARCH 2024 • CHITWAN, NEPAL

Women's Empowerment & Skill Workshop

In celebration of Women's Day, we partnered with a local NGO in Chitwan to host a skill development workshop. We provided training on creating and marketing natural products, empowering women with tools for financial independence while promoting sustainable practices.

Read More →
NOVEMBER 2023 • NAGARKOT

Annual Green Drive Initiative

Our team and volunteers gathered in Nagarkot for our annual tree plantation drive. Together, we planted over 500 native saplings, contributing to the reforestation of local hillsides and reinforcing our commitment to a greener, healthier planet for future generations.

View Gallery →
JULY 2023 • GORKHA, NEPAL

Community Health & Wellness Camp

KeshAayu organized a free health and wellness camp in a remote village in Gorkha. We offered consultations on hair and scalp health using natural remedies, distributed our organic products, and raised awareness about holistic well-being through Ayurvedic principles.
